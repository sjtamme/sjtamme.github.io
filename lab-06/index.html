Map of the Appalachian Trail using the classic map by Erwin Raisz.


Link to high resolution map

Authored by Sarah Jane Tamme at New Maps Plus!

This map was designed on November 22, 2020 with QGIS 3.16 on Windows 10, version 2004. The CRS used was North America Albers Equal Area Conic (EPSG: 102008). The base map is copyright Erwin Raisz. The shapefile of the Appalachian Trail was provided by Appalachian Trail Conservancy. The spatial data for graticules was provided by Natural Earth, and the spatial data for US states was provided by US Census Bureau.

The Appalachian Trail passes through 14 states. Explore the trail by state with the Appalachian Trail Conservancy.
