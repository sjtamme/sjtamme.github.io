Gender Equity Success in Kenyan Primary Schools

This map uses data on Kenyan national boys' and girls' enrollments per class for primary education.

Gender Equality

Kenya has progressive policy frameworks to improve gender equality and equity. Kenya introduced a free primary education policy in 2003. The Constitution of Kenya 2010 also promised the opportunity of education to all Kenyans.
